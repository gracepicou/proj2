Greenville, South Carolina

Greenville, SC is a charming city located in the upstate region of South Carolina. It's known for its beautiful downtown area, picturesque parks, and vibrant arts scene. Some popular attractions in Greenville include Falls Park on the Reedy, the Peace Center for the Performing Arts, and the Greenville Zoo. The city also offers a variety of outdoor acitivies, delicious restaurants, and unique shops.
